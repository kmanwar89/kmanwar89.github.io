Hi there! My name is Kadar (pronounced like 'Cotter' and I'm a network engineer at Cisco.

During the day, I support Enterprise Route, Switch and Wireless networks for a global banking customer in 80+ countries.

At night, I spend my evenings doing whatever piques my interest - it may be riding my motorcycle, playing a video game or even talking long walks with my awesome dog, but more often than not, I will find myself working on some geeky project or another. I view this as both an investment in myself, and a marketable, employable set of skills to use in the future.

After more than a decade accumulating various tidbits of knowledge across all the projects I've done, I thought it was time to write it all down! I found the Simple CSS template and fell in love.
